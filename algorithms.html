Scheduling Algorithms in One Place

Each algorithm now has its own page that combines the live simulation, the mathematical definition, the plain-language interpretation, and the example execution table in one focused lesson.

01

FIFO / FCFS

Arrival-order service with simple behavior and visible convoy effects.
02

Strict Priority

Urgency-first scheduling with strong protection for critical traffic and starvation risk for low classes.
03

Round Robin

Cyclic service that emphasizes queue-level fairness through repeated turns.
04

Weighted Fair Queueing

Weighted fairness using virtual finish tags and long-run service shares.
05

Deficit Round Robin

Credit-based fairness designed to work efficiently with variable-size packets.
06

Earliest Deadline First

Deadline-aware scheduling for traffic where lateness is the key concern.
